Mindset

Without a positive and strong mindset, it's almost impossible to hunt your time well.

Mindset

What's the mindset you must acquire to hunt your time?
  • Stacy
  • May 12, 2021
  • 10 Comments

The difference between us and other creatures are our mind. If we can't control our mind well, we could never control our time well. Hence, we must know the mindset of time management in order to hunt the time. First, aim for continuous development and enjoy your victories along the way. Count all of your time as usable time and strive to find pleasure in every moment.

Most importantly, find something to appreciate in whatever you do. Always attempt to be an optimist and look for the positive in your life and find methods to capitalise on your achievements.



"There is always enough time for the essential things," remind yourself. If it's critical, you'll do it.



To reinforce your objectives, post reminders at your house, workplace, vehicle, or on your phone, computer, planner, or binder. Examine previous behaviours and look for methods to improve them in order to achieve your objectives more effectively. Reward yourself for finishing work or study hours on time, particularly if the accomplishment is significant or challenging.



Be conscious of your self-regulation abilities. Seek to put off short-term satisfaction in order to achieve long-term objectives. Rather of fretting, concentrate on doing action. Create objectives, stick to them, and then trust the method you devised to alleviate anxiety and stress.

If you feel overwhelmed while accomplishing tons of works, it's time to stop and remind yourself on "focus", "find methods" and "stop procastinating". These three words are the most central element for mindset in time management. What's the meaning of focus, find method and stops procastinating?
