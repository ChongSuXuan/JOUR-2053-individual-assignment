5 Best Free Time Management App
  • Stacy
  • May 12, 2021
  • 10 Comments

Time management is the ability to efficiently use your time to complete any activity. When it comes to manage time, we need to manage our daily, weekly and yearly schedule well. By controlling your time, you will be able to maintain a work-life balance. It's all about your ultimate decisions when it comes to effective time management.You may use time management applications to make to-do lists and prioritise activities, set alerts and reminders, organise files and tasks, and facilitate communication among team members. To achieve more effective time management, these 5 free time management application will be your useful toos to assist you in determining where your time is being squandered. The time management software you choose is entirely dependent on your needs, as there are many different sorts of apps that may help you manage your time. Some applications are for businesses, some are for making to-do lists, while yet others are just for keeping track of time.

Importance of Time Management App

  • Reduce Your Stress
  • Plan Ahead
  • Improve Your Performance
  • Increasing Production and Efficiency

5 Free Time Management Application

  1. Todoist




    2. Todoist can assist you in managing everything from one location. It is compatible with all devices. It has the capability of establishing objectives and visualising progress. With a premium membership, you will get access to additional features such as automated reminders, template creation, and data backup.



    Features:
    1. You may establish daily and weekly objectives.
    2. You can see your progress.
    3. It will enable you to go through your accomplished chores.
    4. Todoist can backup your files automatically.
    5. It will enable you to build your own project templates.
    6. Reminders that are sent automatically.


  2. Monday.com




    3. Monday.com offers a time management tool. You will be able to manage and monitor time in a single application. The timer may be simply turned on and off. It will enable you to manually input the time. Monday.com may be connected with your preferred tool. It has a smartphone app for tracking the time anywhere, at any time.



    Features:
    1. You have the option of manually entering the time.
    2. It can be quickly integrated with your preferred tool.
    3. A smartphone app will allow you to monitor the time from anywhere at any time.
    4. It has a versatile reporting tool that allows you to obtain time breakdowns by projects, clients, and tasks.


  3. Time Doctor




    4. Time Doctor is a customised time monitoring app that works on all platforms, including iOS and Android. By encrypting the data, it ensures its privacy and security. Time Doctor may monitor offline data and then sync it to your account whenever you're connected to the internet.



    Features:
    1. It will allow you to manually change the time.
    2. You can connect the application with existing project management and budgeting platforms like as Slack, Wrike, and others.
    3. It tracks productivity and generates summary reports.


  4. Clockify Time App




    5. Clockify is a free time management application. It is a web-based tool that can be used for time tracking on both desktops and mobile devices. It is available as a desktop programme for Windows and Mac. It is available as an app for Android, iPhone, and iPad.



    Features:
    1. Reports will offer information on workers and their responsibilities. It can assist you in calculating customer bills as well as staff compensation.
    2. The reports may be exported in PDF, CSV, and Excel formats.
    3. You will be able to add an infinite number of users.
    4. Multiple accounts or workplaces are possible.
    5. You will be able to configure the tool's permissions.
    6. It will enable you to organise users for simpler user administration.
    7. It will aid you in project management.


  5. RescueTime




    6. RescueTime is a time management programme that will monitor your computer activity and give you with statistics. It will assist you in being more productive. It will enable you to stop and resume time tracking. It provides the same degree of security as a bank.



    Features:
    • It will enable you to generate notification alerts.
    • It can monitor offline activities such as meetings and phone conversations.
    • With a free plan, it can offer three months of report history and an infinite report history with a premium plan.
    • It will enable you to block websites in order to prevent distractions and stay focused on achieving your objectives.

  • Suitable for your lifestyle
  • Easily access from any devices
